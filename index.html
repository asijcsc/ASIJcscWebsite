About Us

The ASIJ CSC club is for those who love to learn anything about computer science, in this club, you'll be able to learn any niche you want and create projects based on that.

Why Computer Science?

High paying jobs

The starting average salary for a computer scientist is $130k a year, according to the US Bureau of Labor Statistics.

Problem-solving

Computer Science teaches you how to think critically, and solve complex problems. Learn how to build innovate solutions, automate tasks, and develop new technologies.

Impressive Portfolio

Because of the transferable skills and versatility of programming, if you work on coding projects, create web apps, and do other endeavours, it will look very impressive on your college app.
